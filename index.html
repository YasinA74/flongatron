Who We Are

The Manifesto

FlongaTron is an indie creations studio that operates by one rule: build what you want, ship it, and let the world decide. No committees. Pure creative instinct turned into playable reality.

Every game is built to travel. Designed for global audiences from day one, to be discovered on any screen, in any market, in any language. If you play it, it was made for you.

The studio was built in a region the games industry has largely overlooked. The Global South is home to the next wave of digital entertainment: hundreds of millions of players, rising creators, and communities ready for experiences that reflect their world.
We're building for the world as it actually is.
