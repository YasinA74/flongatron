FlongaTron Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

FlongaTron (“we”, “our”, or “us”) develops and publishes mobile applications and games on platforms including Google Play and other distribution channels.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our applications.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Contact information (e.g., email for support)
  • Feedback, messages, or other communications

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system, device identifiers)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (interactions, session duration, features used)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

This information may be collected directly or through third-party SDKs integrated into the app.

2. Use of Information

  • Operate and maintain our applications
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Monitor usage and analytics
  • Provide support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

3. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics for Firebase.

These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data and interactions

More info: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising

Our applications may include third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob or similar providers).

  • Collect device identifiers and usage data
  • Use cookies or similar technologies
  • Provide personalized or non-personalized ads

Where required, user consent will be requested.

5. In-App Purchases (IAP)

Our applications may offer in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.

All transactions are processed securely by Google. We do not store payment details.

More info: Google Privacy Policy

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

  • Service providers (analytics, ads, infrastructure)
  • Legal authorities if required
  • Operational partners

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary unless legally required otherwise.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no system is 100% secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our apps may be accessible to children depending on the experience.

We comply with applicable laws regarding children's data and do not knowingly collect data unlawfully.

If a child’s data has been submitted improperly, contact us at legal@flonga.org.

Deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

11. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion
  • Object to certain processing

12. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed outside your country with different data protection laws.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.
